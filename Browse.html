Dubai Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried kataifi
  • 1/2 cup pistachio cream
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs white chocolate, melted
  • 1 cup dark chocolate, melted

Instructions

  1. 1.Get an 8×4 inch loaf pan ready and add parchment paper.
  2. 2.In a small pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add in the kataifi and stir continuously until golden brown for about 5 minutes. Place toasted kataifi into a bowl along with pistachio cream and mix until combined.
  3. 3.In the loaf pan, drizzle the melted WHITE chocolate in a zigzag pattern and refrigerate for 5 minutes. Next pour half of the melted dark chocolate into the loaf pan and spread around the loaf pan evenly, then refrigerate 10 minutes. After, transfer the kataifi mixture to the pan and spread out evenly. Top with remaining dark chocolate and spread out evenly. Refrigerate for 3 hours or until set. Enjoy the chocolate!

Frozen gummy bears

Ingredients

  • Sprite Soda
  • Gummy Bears

Instructions

  1. 1.Place the gummy bears in a container with each gummy bear having space from the ones next to it. Pour the Sprite Soda all over the gummy bears

  3. 3.Be careful not to break the gummy bears. Keep the Sprite soaked gummy bears in the freezer for 3 hours.

No Bake Cheesecake

Ingredients

    Crust

  • 8 ¾ ounces of graham crackers
  • 2 ½ ounces of melted, unsalted butter
  • A pinch of kosher salt (For taste)

    • Filling

  • 16 ounces of plain, full fat cream cheese
  • 5 ¼ ounces of sugar
  • ¾ ounce of fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 12 ounces of heavy cream

Instructions

    For the Crust

  1. 1.Crush the graham crackers into crumbs and mix with the butter in a small bowl. If you decide to add salt, go ahead and add that too. Spread it into a pie plate and press it into a firm, even layer across the bottom and sides of the plate. Refrigerate until needed.

    2. For the Filling

  2. 1.Combine the cream cheese, sugar, lemon juice, vanilla, and salt in a bowl and mix at a low speed until the mixture forms a thick paste, then increase the speed until soft and smooth.
  3. 2.Add the heavy cream. We recommend using an electric mixer for best results. Mix at a slow speed to combine, then increase the speed until the mixture can hold stiff peaks.
  4. 3.Pour the filling into the crust that you prepared earlier and refrigerate until the filling is cold and firm, then top with fruit to add an extra bit of sweetness!

Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, giblets removed
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder, or to taste
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 stalk celery, leaves removed

Instructions

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. 2. Place chicken in a roasting pan and season generously inside and out with onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. 3. Place 3 tablespoons of butter in the chicken cavity; arrange dollops of remaining butter on the outside of the chicken. Then cut your celery into 3 or 4 pieces then place inside the chicken cavity.
  4. 4. Bake chicken in a preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes. (until the chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear).
  5. 5. Remove the chicken from the oven and baste with juices. Rest the chicken with aluminum foil for about 30 minutes before serving.

One Pot Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dry uncooked pasta, gluten-free or regular
  • 8 ounces grape or cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small zucchini, chopped and quartered
  • 3 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cup pasta sauce of choice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3 ounces fresh spinach

Instructions

    For the Crust

  1. 1. Add the pasta to a large pot as well as the other ingredients except for the spinach.
  2. Bring the pot to a boil. After boiling, reduce heat to medium low and cook 10-14 minutes. Stir the pasta every 2 minutes
  3. Put the toppings and cook until it's done, then place the sauce into the pot.
  4. Turn off the heat and fold spinach into the mixture. After spinach has wilted, divide pasta to serve. Enjoy!
